SHARP PRIVACY POLICY

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Sharp (“Application”) for mobile devices that was created by GooseByte. The Application is a game in which players are required to solve math equations as quickly as possible. 

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

User Provided Information 

No user provided information is collected by the Application.

Automatically Collected Information 

No information is automatically collected by the Application. 

Does the Application collect precise real time location information of the device?

No.

Do third parties see and/or have access to information obtained by the Application?

No.

Why is there a privacy policy if no data is collected by the Application?

The application requires an Android permission called GET_ACCOUNTS which allows the application to integrate to Google Play Games services. This is required to connect to your gamer profile on your Android device which in turn enables the global leader board.

Children

We do not use the Application to knowingly solicit data from or market to children under the age of 13. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that his or her child has provided us with information without their consent, he or she should contact us at info@goosebyte.com. We will delete such information from our files within a reasonable time.

Contact us

If you have any questions regarding privacy while using the Application, or have questions about our practices, please contact us via email at info@goosebyte.com.
